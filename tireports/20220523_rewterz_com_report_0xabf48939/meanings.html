  • The BazarLoader malware is a backdoor or malware that is installed on a Windows host that has been compromised. Bazar Loader has targeted a wide range of enterprises in North America and Europe since its launch in April 2020. BazarLoader presently employs a BazarCall technique to infect the victims machine and give attackers backdoors that can be used to deliver follow-up malware, monitor the environment, and target more susceptible hosts on the network in the future. The malware was discovered as part of the Team9 malware family, which was linked to the Trickbot development team.

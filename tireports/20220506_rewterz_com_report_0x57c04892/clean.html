Analysis Summary

Sidewinder is a suspected Indian threat actor group that has been active since 2012. They have been observed attacking political, military, and corporate organizations throughout Asia, with Pakistan, China, Nepal, and Afghanistan being the most common targets. RAZOR TIGER, Rattlesnake, APT-C-17, and T-APT-04 are the aliases for Sidewinder APT. This APT has been targeting Pakistani government officials with a decoy file related to FOCUSED TALK ON RUSSIAN UKRAINE CONFLICT IMPACT ON PAKISTAN in its most recent effort. They employ custom implementations to attack existing vulnerabilities and then deploy a Powershell payload in the final stages to distribute the malware.

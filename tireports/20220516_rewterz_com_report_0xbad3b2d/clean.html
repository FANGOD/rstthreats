Analysis Summary

CVE-2022-28837

Adobe Acrobat and Adobe Reader could allow a remote attacker to obtain sensitive information, caused by a use-after-free within the handling of Doc objects. By persuading a victim to open a specially-crafted document, a remote attacker could exploit this vulnerability to obtain sensitive information.

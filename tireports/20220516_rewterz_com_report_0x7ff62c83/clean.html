Analysis Summary

CVE-2022-27640 CVSS:7.4

Siemens SIMATIC CP 442-1 and CP 443-1 RNA is vulnerable to a denial of service, caused by improperly handling excessive ARP broadcast requests. By sending numerous ARP requests, a remote attacker could exploit this vulnerability to cause a denial of service.

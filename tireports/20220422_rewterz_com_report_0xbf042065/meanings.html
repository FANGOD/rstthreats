References:
Titles (2)

  • Analysis Summary
Sentences (1)
  • Grandoreiro is a globally widespread malware and uses modular installers to evade detection. The malware makes use of the victims privileges and access to perform fraudulent banking transactions. This helps them evade the security measures used by banking institutions. A specific DGA (Domain Generation Algorithm) is used by the malware to hide the CnC addresses used during an attack. Grandoreiro follows a Malware-as-a-Service (MaaS) business model and is operated by many cybercrime groups. The malware is mainly used to target Brazillian and European Banks. The cluster targeting Brazil used hacked websites and Google Ads to drive users to download the malicious installer. The campaign targeting other countries used spear-phishing as the delivery method.

Xloader is distributing via spoofed emails containing malicious file attachments of Microsoft documents and infecting about 69 countries. between December 1, 2020, and June 1, 2021, with 53% of the infections reported in the U.S. alone, followed by Chinas special administrative regions (SAR), Mexico, Germany, and France.
